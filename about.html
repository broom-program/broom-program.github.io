About

BROOM (Building Resolve and Opportunity for Oncoming MOPpers) is a collaborative, highly intensive online math program modeled after MOP, open to students entering 9th grade and above. Running for the first time this summer, the program is designed by many past and current MOPpers to bring the MOP experience to everyone.

Logistics:

Dates: June 11th to July 2nd.
Duration: 6-10 hours.
Location: a MineCraft Server for an immersive experience.
Tests: 3 practice tests, 2 ELMO-style tests, and 3 graded TSTST-style tests.
Cost: only 90 dollars (much cheaper than other programs/camps) (financial aid is available)
Registration: right here.
